# Overview

A web app where people keep track of their tasks. Each task can have a due date and belong to a category.

# Who uses it

- Individual users — manage their personal to-do lists.

# What they can do

- Someone creates a new task with a title, optional due date, and optional category.
- They mark tasks as complete or incomplete.
- They edit a task's title, due date, or category.
- They delete tasks they no longer need.
- They see all their tasks in one place.
